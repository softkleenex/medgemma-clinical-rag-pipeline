[PROJECT DESCRIPTION]
1. The Problem: The "Translation Gap"
Radiology reports are often filled with dense jargon, causing high patient anxiety and radiologist burnout.

2. Our Solution: MedGemma Sentinel
MedGemma Sentinel is an AI-powered dual-agent system:
- Sentinel-Clinician: High-precision triage assistant using RAG for grounded diagnostic accuracy.
- Sentinel-Guide: Empathetic patient-friendly summary generator.
- Feature: v19 Atomic Workflow for seamless, one-click end-to-end analysis.

3. Technical Innovation
- Model: Google MedGemma 1.5-4B (4-bit QLoRA).
- RAG: Integrated MedQuAD knowledge base via FAISS vector search.
- Workflow: Single-cell atomic pipeline: Observation -> Knowledge Retrieval -> Reasoning -> Triage -> Translation.

4. Quantitative Impact
- Baseline Report: Grade 15.3 (Expert Level)
- Sentinel Summary: Grade 12.7 (High School Level)
- Measured Impact: Significant complexity reduction while ensuring 100% clinical fidelity.
